About Us

Our Mission

The Chamber's Mission is to actively promote its members and their business activities while simultaneously working to enhance the quality of life for the residents, stakeholders and visitors to Pleasure Island. The mission will be achieved through the promotion of member businesses, destination, marketing, tourism promotions and the creation of sustainable economic development, initiatives for Carolina and Kure Beach, Ft. Fisher and contiguous communities.
